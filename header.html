Game instruction

Access the Game: Go to the online Bingo website or app.


Select Bingo Variant: Choose a game with numbers from 1 to 75.


Buy a Card: Purchase a single Bingo card.


Watch for Numbers: Pay attention as numbers are called out.


Mark Numbers: If a called number matches yours, mark it on your card.


Complete a Line: Aim to fill a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line.


Call Bingo: When you complete a line, click "Bingo" or announce your win.


Collect Your Prize: If you're the first to complete a line, you win!


Restart or Exit: Play another round or leave the game as desired.

RULES

Get Your Card: Start with a single Bingo card containing numbers 1-75.


Listen for Calls: Pay attention as numbers are called out randomly.


Mark Your Card: If a called number matches one on your card, mark it.


Complete a Line: Aim to fill a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line on your card.


Call Bingo: When you complete a line, shout "Bingo" or click the "Bingo" button.


Verify Win: The game will confirm if you're the first to complete a line.


Collect Prize: If you win, you'll receive a prize.


Restart or Exit: Play again or leave the game as you like.
